Why Choose Us

In this project, we developed a deep learning model using TensorFlow to predict the direction of Bitcoin's open price for the next day. Rather than employing traditional architectures like LSTM or RNN, we opted for a fully connected neural network (FCNN) with Leaky ReLU activation functions. This architecture leverages the power of dense layers to capture the intricate patterns in Bitcoin price movements.


Our model's architecture consists of multiple fully connected layers with Leaky ReLU activations to handle the non-linearity and prevent the vanishing gradient problem. The final output layer utilizes a sigmoid activation function to provide a probabilistic prediction on whether the next day's Bitcoin open price will be higher or lower compared to today's.


We trained and validated the model on historical Bitcoin price data, achieving an impressive 98% accuracy and a high F1 score on the test dataset. These metrics indicate that our model is highly effective in predicting the direction of Bitcoin prices, offering valuable insights for traders and investors.
